How to Import into Minecraft:

  1. Download the generated sculpture.mcfunction file.
  2. Place it in your world folder: .minecraft/saves/YOUR_WORLD/datapacks/YOUR_DATAPACK/data/custom/function/
  3. In-game, stand where you want the structure's bottom-left corner and run:
    /function custom:sculpture
